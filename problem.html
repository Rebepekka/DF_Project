Welcome to the Kotilo problem-solving section. Here you will find a set of problems aimed at improving waste management and promoting better environmental practices. Each problem is designed to challenge your creativity and help you think of innovative solutions.

By participating, you are not only helping the environment but also contributing to a larger movement toward sustainable living. Explore the problems below and take the first step towards making a difference.

Waste bins

Proper waste sorting is essential for recycling, yet many struggle with separation, leading to contamination. Clearly labeled bins help educate consumers, reduce contamination, and improve recycling efficiency.

Waste shed/area

Plastic pollution is a major environmental issue. Designated waste sheds for plastic disposal can encourage responsible management and ensure proper recycling, reducing harm to ecosystems and health.

Accessibility

Organic waste contributes significantly to landfills and greenhouse gas emissions. Enhancing access to composting systems and educating communities can promote organic waste diversion and support a healthier environment.
